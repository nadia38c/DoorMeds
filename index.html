About DoorMeds

Who We Are and What We Do

Door-to-Door Health Solutions Provider

DoorMeds is an All-Integrated Door-to-Door Health Solutions Provider to make your life easy and healthy. By creating a DoorMeds account, you'll have entire pharmacies and medical stores in your fingertips, plus services such as Nursing and Physiotherapy from the comfort of your home.


It is a Must-Have App for -

  • Every health conscious person.
  • People who want to save time.
  • People who want a One stop solution to their Health Needs.
  • Smart people who want to save Money.

our story

An untold story of DoorMeds.

DoorMeds is a story. A story of 5 young, confident and dreamy people. The dream was never making money. The dream is always about making a self-identity as an entrepreneur. An identity that will entitle them with the blessings of doing something for the society, for the economy of the nation and for the betterment of mankind. The process started with a small concept of delivering required medicines to the home of people that eventually has taken the shape of all integrated door to door health solution provider!
The starting was not smooth. We couldn’t even imagine good things coming so fast! We didn't even had a chair & table of our own to make a meeting. We had no other staff and used to deliver by ourselves. But the ALMIGHTY showed his blessings to the determination of us. Our service earned a good response and we started thinking of something bigger. We knew no bound to express our joy when we took our first office and a small showroom of medicines. These were small but our own! We didn’t stop. With more confidence, we kept continuing our service with people’s love, suggestions & support. We became able to make our position even stronger and became the first ONLINE TECH START-UP to have their own big showroom of medicines! And now, ALHAMDULILLAH, it’s a great pleasure of us to announce the launching of our very own DoorMeds App right on the month of celebrating our 1st anniversary!
We dedicate this achievement to our valued customers, guardians & well wishers for being with us all the time!
The journey is still young & tender. We promise our customers to serve with more integrity, passion and determination to establish the new concept of all integrated door to door health solutions for all!

Frequently Asked questions

Here are some commonly asked question.

1. Is DoorMeds a Pharmacy?

>

<

No. DoorMeds is a Health Solutions Provider. The goal of DoorMeds is to create a platform to make Health solutions more accessible, and affordable for its users, through its Mobile App. DoorMeds owns a Pharmacy, and is partnered with several Pharmacies and Health Institutions in Sylhet City, to give the best experience for the users.


2. What areas do DoorMeds provide Service?

>

<

Currently, we operate only on the Sylhet City, and looking forward to expand all over Bangladesh In Sha Allah.


3. How can I contact the Deliveryman?

>

<

After you place an order, a DoorMeds operator will contact you within 10 minutes to confirm your order and give you an estimated delivery time.


4. What if I get a wrong delivery?

>

<

You can contact our operators and we will change the order for you, with no hassle!


5. How can I know my total bill?

>

<

You can see your total bill at the checkout when you order. Plus our Operator will also call you to confirm the price, and if there’s any changes.


6. Do I get the medicines from the manufacturer/company that I requested?

>

<

Yes, sure. We will deliver from the company you request. However, if that medicine is unavailable, our operator will inform you and take your permission to deliver from a different company.


7. What if I get overcharged?

>

<

You can contact us for a hassle-free refund!


8. How can I pay?

>

<

Currently two options: Cash On Delivery, and BKash.


9. Do you offer discounts?

>

<

Yes, we do, and it can vary anywhere from 5% to 10% depending on the Order value. The more you order, the higher the discount.


10. Do you have any delivery charge?

>

<

We charge a very modest fee of 25 taka per order to cover the fees that we pay our delivery personnel.


11. My deliveryman misbehaved with me. Where do I complain?

>

<

We deal with these matters very seriously. Go to the complaint box of your app, and file a complaint. We will take serious actions.


12. The product I want is not available in your cart. What do I do?

>

<

You can text us the name of the product, and we will manually check whether we can offer it to you.


13. Where do you source products from and how do you ensure quality?

>

<

We are partnered with registered pharmacies and regulated top grade manufacturers of the country. Plus we also have a Quality Assurance Center in Shibgonj, Sylhet to make sure we deliver the highest quality products.


14. How do you make profit even by giving discounts and

<

>

>

Our margins are very small, but that’s okay because we have a lot of users. Profit per Order is low, but when there are a lot of orders, we can cover up our costs. Our goal is to give the highest quality of service at the most affordable rate.


15. How do I trust you with my medicines? Do you have any licenses?

>

<

We are fully licensed from the Government of Bangladesh. Plus you can visit our office directly to contact our representatives.
